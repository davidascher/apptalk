Motivation

Why should you build a webby app?

  • Freedom
  • Open Market
  • Huge Market
  • Think Bigger

The web is universal: everyone can play

Free as in freedom for developers & entrepreneurs.

No censor to decide whether your app is acceptable or not.

No platform vendor to say your app competes with theirs.

Open to New Competitors

The current world reinforces a short tail of winners

Platform vendors (Facebook, Apple, ...)

Tier 1 players with internal network effects (Zynga, etc.)

Platforms happy to reinforce leaders

Imagine a world with 1000s of appstores.

Imagine social networks competing for app recommendations.

No platform vendor to say your app competes with theirs.

The World is your Market

iOS market is smaller than total market

Facebook market is smaller than total market

Any one market is smaller than total market

Think Bigger

If your social app requires facebook, it doesn't map to people's real social networks

If your gaming app works on iOS, it only targets the wealthy

If your app isn't accessible, you're losing customers (or entire markets)

Because we've been here before, and it ends in tears.
